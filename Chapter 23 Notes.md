**Measuring Current**:
- Ammeter: 
	- Schematic symbol: -(A)-
	- Ideal: 0 $\ohm$

**Measuring Voltage**:
- Voltmeter:
	- Symbol: -(V)-
	- Ideal: $\infty$ resistance
	- Connected in parallel
